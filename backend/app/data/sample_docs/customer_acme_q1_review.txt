Acme Corp - Q1 2024 Business Review

Customer: Acme Corporation
Product: Project Management Suite
Period: January 1 - March 31, 2024

Overview: Acme Corp showed strong engagement with the core project planning features. Adoption rates for task assignment and progress tracking increased by 15% this quarter.

Key Challenges:
1.  **Integration with legacy CRM**: Persistent issues reported with data synchronization between our suite and their existing CRM system. This caused delays in client reporting for their sales team.
2.  **Reporting Customization**: Users requested more flexible and customizable reporting dashboards, specifically for cross-project budget allocation. Current templates were deemed too rigid for their complex financial tracking needs.
3.  **Onboarding feedback**: Initial onboarding for new team members was slow due to insufficient self-service documentation. CSM provided additional one-on-one sessions.

Achievements:
*   Successfully launched three major projects using the platform.
*   Reduced internal meeting times by 20% due to better task visibility.

Next Steps:
*   Schedule a deep-dive with engineering for CRM integration issues.
*   Gather detailed requirements for custom reporting features.
*   Develop a new self-service onboarding guide.
