Customer: Globex Corporation
Product: Cloud Analytics Platform
Date: April 12, 2024
Source: Monthly Check-in Call with John Doe (CTO)

Overall Sentiment: Positive, but with specific areas for improvement.

Impact: Latency issues sometimes disrupt their daily reporting schedule.

Action Items:
